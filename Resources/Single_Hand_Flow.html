Mermaid Diagram Preview

    flowchart TD
    A[Start New Hand] --> B[Reset pot to 0]
    B --> C[Shuffle and Deal Cards]
    C --> D[Assign Small and Big Guess]
    D --> E[Prediction Round 1 - Pre-Flop]
    E --> F{Only 1 player remains}
    F -- Yes --> G[Give pot to last player] --> Z[End Hand]
    F -- No --> H[Deal 3 Community Cards - Flop]
    H --> I[Prediction Round 2 - Post-Flop]
    I --> J{Only 1 player remains}
    J -- Yes --> G
    J -- No --> K[Deal 4th Community Card - Turn]
    K --> L[Prediction Round 3 - Post-Turn]
    L --> M{Only 1 player remains}
    M -- Yes --> G
    M -- No --> N[Deal 5th Community Card - River]
    N --> O[Final Prediction Round]
    O --> P{Only 1 player remains}
    P -- Yes --> G
    P -- No --> Q[Evaluate Hands of All Active Players]
    Q --> R[Determine Winning Hand or Tie]
    R --> S[Distribute Pot to Winner or Split]
    S --> T[Update Player Credits]
    T --> U[Eliminate Players with 0 Credits]
    U --> Z[End Hand]
